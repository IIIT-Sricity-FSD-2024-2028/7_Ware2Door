Shipping & Tracking

Visit the Track Order page and enter your Tracking ID (e.g. BLR78291XQ). You'll see a live status timeline covering Warehouse → Transit Hub → Local Delivery → Delivered.

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express shipments are delivered within 24–48 hours. Timelines may vary based on your pin code and product category.

Address changes are only possible before the shipment leaves the warehouse. Please submit a support ticket immediately with your Tracking ID and new address details.

Returns & RTO

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Initiate a return by submitting a ticket under the "Return to Origin" category with photos of the issue.

A Return-to-Origin (RTO) is automatically triggered after 3 failed delivery attempts. To manually request an RTO, raise a support ticket with your Tracking ID before the shipment is out for delivery again.

There are no charges for the first failed delivery attempt. Subsequent re-attempt fees depend on your shipment plan. RTO processing may attract a nominal handling fee as per your service agreement.
